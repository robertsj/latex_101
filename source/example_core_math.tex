\section{Math Examples}

Inline math: $\alpha, \beta, \Gamma, \delta$.

Displayed math:
\[
\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{1}{x}=0.
\]

Matrix:
\[
\begin{matrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{matrix}
\]

Cases:
\[
f(x)=
\begin{cases}
x^2, & x\ge0, \\
-x^2, & x<0.
\end{cases}
\]
